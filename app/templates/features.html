How to Use

How does one simply use THE FUTURE?

1.

Download a simple image (with white background and simple contours) in jpg or png format. See Gallery for examples.

2.

Upload the image and set the base length to the number of characters you want on the base.

3.

Click the "Time Travel" button and be prepared to witness the future in all of its glory.
